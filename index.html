The Best Game Ever!

Shiny Hunting Pokemon Simulator is a Pokemon game with increased odds.

When you encounter a pokemon you can press the catch button until it says catch on your screen than keep running around trying to find shiny pokemon!

    Controls:
  • W: walking forwards
  • A: Walk Left
  • S: Walk Backwards
  • D: Walk Right

Battle controls:
Run button: Leave the battle
Catch button: Throw a Pokeball to see if you catch the pokemon

Once you have encountered a shiny pokemon you can click it and it will be added to your list of shiny pokemon!

Have fun!
